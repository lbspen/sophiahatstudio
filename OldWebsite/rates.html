Rates

How much does it cost?

Isn't that what everything comes down to?

We charge a flat rate of  $40/hour for all services.  12 hour days can be reserved for $360. Please inquire about special rates for larger or ongoing projects.

All sessions include a free cdr of the work accomplished during the session. Additional copies can be purchased for $3 each.

Limited run duplication services are also available for $5/unit. We use printable Taiyo Yuden cdrs that are smudge proof. The price includes a standard jewel case with 2-panel insert and traycard with one spine. The minimum order is 20.

Data DVDs of the session are $5 each.

Before booking we recommend that you read our Studio Policies so that you are in the know.
