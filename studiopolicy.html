Studio Policies

The fine print

Our rules are simple, straight forward and to the point.

no bubblegum chewing around the microphone; no peeing in the sink; payment is due every hour on the hour; no garlic or heavily spiced foods on the console (fat free and low sodium products are OK); no running with scissors; no jumping with sodas; no petss; all peanut butter sandwiches must have jel;ly; shoes must be worn at all times except during mixing sessions of longer duration (5 hrs or more);

 

Here it is...

All clients are encouraged to bring a hard drive for data backup. We can burn data DVDs of your project, but it is far less reliable than a external hard drive. Although we will maintain at least one copy of the client's active project, we cannot assume responsibility for the clients data. That's why it is important for the client to keep a copy for themselves. We clear our drives once the project is over.

Payments are due at the end of a session unless another arrangement has been made prior to the session commencement.

Bookings that are cancelled within 48 hrs of the start are subject to a $50 termination fee.
