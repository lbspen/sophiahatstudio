Tom Prather

Primarily self-taught, Tom has been playing music and recording for over 20 years. He has recently refined and enhanced his skills in the Music Technology program at Shoreline CC. His projects have covered all sorts of genres including rock, jazz, orchestral, singer/songwriter, dance and electronic. Tom is also the resident MIDI guru and has a strong background in audio synthesis and sound manipulation. He's extremely proficient in Digital Performer, as well as Pro Tools. Focused on details, Tom takes pride in providing a professional product while remaining flexible to the clients needs.
